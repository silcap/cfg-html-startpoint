Tiramisù

  • ⏱ PREP TIME: 5 mins
  • ⏱ COOK TIME: 15 mins
  • 🍴 SERVES: 4

    • Tiramisù is a popular coffee-flavoured Italian dessert made of ladyfingers dipped in coffee, layered with a whipped mixture of eggs, sugar, and mascarpone cheese, flavoured with cocoa. The origins of this recipe, whose name means "pick me up", "cheer me up" or "lift me up", are often disputed among Italian regions of Veneto, Friuli-Venezia Giulia, Piedmont, and others.
